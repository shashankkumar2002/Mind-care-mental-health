About Mental Health

Mental health is a critical component of overall well-being that affects how we think, feel, and act. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.

Our mission is to provide accessible resources, reduce stigma, and help individuals understand their mental health better through education and self-assessment tools.

Remember: seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Everyone deserves support and understanding on their mental health journey.

Anxiety Level Assessment

This questionnaire is based on common anxiety symptoms. It's for educational purposes and not a substitute for professional diagnosis.

1. How often do you feel nervous, anxious, or on edge?

2. How often are you unable to stop or control worrying?

3. How often do you worry too much about different things?

4. How often do you have trouble relaxing?

5. How often are you so restless that it's hard to sit still?

6. How often do you feel afraid that something awful might happen?
